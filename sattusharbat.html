Sattu Sharbat - Traditional Bihari Drink

Refreshing Sattu Sharbat Recipe

Sattu Sharbat is a nutritious and cooling drink made from roasted gram flour (sattu). It is extremely popular in Bihar, especially during the summer for its energizing and refreshing properties.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • 2 cups chilled water
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or jaggery (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • A pinch of black salt
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix sattu with a little water to make a smooth paste without lumps.
  2. Add the remaining water slowly, stirring continuously.
  3. Mix in lemon juice, sugar or jaggery, roasted cumin powder, and black salt.
  4. Stir everything well until completely dissolved.
  5. Pour into glasses, garnish with mint leaves if desired, and serve chilled.
