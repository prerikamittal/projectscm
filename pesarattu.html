Classic Pesarattu Recipe

Pesarattu is a delicious and healthy crepe made from green gram (moong dal). It is a popular Andhra Pradesh breakfast dish, often served with coconut chutney or ginger chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green gram (whole moong dal)
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak green gram overnight or for 6 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the soaked dal with green chilies and ginger into a smooth batter. Add little water if needed.
  3. Mix salt into the batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa and pour a ladleful of batter. Spread it thin like dosa.
  5. Sprinkle chopped onions on top and press them lightly.
  6. Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook until the bottom turns golden brown.
  7. Flip and cook for a minute if needed, then serve hot with chutney!
