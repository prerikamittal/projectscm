Pasta - Italian Delight

Classic Tomato Basil Pasta

This classic Italian pasta combines al dente spaghetti with rich tomato sauce, fresh garlic, and basil leaves. It’s a quick and comforting meal, loved for its simplicity and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in basil leaves and cooked spaghetti. Toss to combine.
  5. Serve hot with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Lasagna - Cheesy Layers of Joy

Classic Beef Lasagna

This traditional Italian lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of mozzarella and Parmesan. A hearty dish perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups marinara or meat sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Add marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers. Top with Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
