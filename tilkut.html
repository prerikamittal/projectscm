Tilkut - Traditional Bihari Sweet

Classic Tilkut Recipe

Tilkut is a traditional Bihari sweet made from sesame seeds (til) and jaggery or sugar. It is especially popular during Makar Sankranti and symbolizes warmth and festivity.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sesame seeds (til)
  • 3/4 cup jaggery or sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (for greasing)

Instructions:

  1. Roast sesame seeds on low heat until they are golden and aromatic. Let them cool slightly.
  2. Grind half the sesame seeds coarsely, keeping the rest whole for texture.
  3. Melt jaggery (or sugar) in a pan to make a thick syrup. Test by dropping a little syrup into water â€” it should harden instantly.
  4. Mix the roasted sesame seeds into the syrup quickly and stir well.
  5. Grease a flat surface or plate with ghee and immediately spread the mixture evenly.
  6. While still warm, cut into desired shapes (rounds or squares).
  7. Allow to cool and harden before serving. Store in an airtight container.
