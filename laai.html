Laai - Traditional Puffed Rice Sweet

Classic Laai Recipe

Laai is a traditional Bihari sweet made from puffed rice (murmura) and jaggery syrup. Itâ€™s a simple, nostalgic treat often enjoyed during festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 cup jaggery (gur)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Optional: crushed peanuts or sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat water and jaggery in a pan to make a thick syrup. Test it by dropping a little in water; it should form a hard ball.
  2. Add ghee to the syrup for flavor.
  3. Quickly mix puffed rice into the hot syrup, stirring well to coat evenly.
  4. Grease your palms with ghee and form small balls (or set into a greased tray and cut squares).
  5. Allow to cool and harden before serving.
