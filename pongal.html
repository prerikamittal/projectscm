Ven Pongal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Cashews (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Dry roast moong dal till aromatic, then wash it with rice.
  2. Pressure cook rice and dal with 4 cups water and salt for 3-4 whistles.
  3. In a pan, heat ghee. Add pepper, cumin, ginger, curry leaves, and cashews.
  4. Fry until golden and pour over the cooked rice-dal mixture.
  5. Mix well and serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
