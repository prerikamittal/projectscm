Traditional South Indian Breakfast

Ven Pongal is a comforting and delicious South Indian dish made with rice, moong dal, and flavored with ghee, black pepper, cumin seeds, and ginger. It's best served hot with coconut chutney or sambar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 tsp black pepper (coarsely crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger (chopped)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (about 4 cups)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Dry roast moong dal until slightly golden. Wash both rice and dal.
  2. Pressure cook rice and dal with 4 cups water until soft.
  3. In a pan, heat ghee. Add crushed pepper, cumin seeds, ginger, and curry leaves.
  4. Add the tempering to the cooked pongal. Mix well.
  5. Add salt, stir and serve hot with chutney or sambar.

Tips

  • Adjust water quantity for desired consistency.
  • Use generous ghee for best taste.
