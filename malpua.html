Malpua - Sweet and Soft Pancake Delight

Classic Malpua Recipe

Malpua is a traditional Indian dessert made of flour, sugar, and milk, deep-fried and soaked in sugar syrup. Popular during festivals like Holi and Diwali.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for syrup)
  • 1/4 cup water (for syrup)

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, milk, and sugar into a smooth batter. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and pour small amounts of batter to form pancakes.
  3. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until slightly thick.
  5. Soak the fried malpuas in the warm syrup for a few minutes.
  6. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with nuts if desired!
