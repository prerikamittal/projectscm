Chole Bhature - North Indian Classic

Classic Chole Bhature Recipe

Chole Bhature is a beloved North Indian dish made with spicy chickpeas (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature). It's a hearty and flavorful meal, often enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas until soft. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Mix in tomato puree, turmeric, and chole masala. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Add chickpeas and some water. Simmer for 15–20 minutes. Garnish with coriander.
  6. For bhature, mix all ingredients to form a soft dough. Rest it for 1-2 hours.
  7. Roll into rounds and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  8. Serve hot bhature with spicy chole and sliced onions or pickle.
