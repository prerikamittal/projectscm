Classic Dosa Recipe

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It's typically served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 3 cups rice
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6 hours.
  2. Grind them to a smooth batter using little water as needed. The batter should be thick but pourable.
  3. Leave the batter to ferment overnight or for 8â€“10 hours in a warm place.
  4. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix gently.
  5. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa, pour a ladle of batter, and spread into a thin circle.
  6. Drizzle oil/ghee around the edges. Cook until golden and crisp. No need to flip.
  7. Serve hot with sambar and chutneys!
