Pizza - Italian Classic

Classic Margherita Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made with a yeasted flatbread topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. Perfectly baked to crispiness, it's a comfort food favorite around the world.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil and salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and warm water in a bowl. Knead until smooth.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  3. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a round base.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base. Add shredded mozzarella and basil leaves on top.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle a pinch of salt.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Slice and serve hot with extra toppings if desired.
