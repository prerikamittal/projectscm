Traditional Ragi Mudde Recipe

Ragi Mudde is a nutritious Karnataka specialty made with finger millet flour. Itâ€™s soft, healthy, and best enjoyed with spicy sambar or saaru!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ragi (finger millet) flour
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a deep heavy-bottomed pan with a pinch of salt.
  2. Reserve a little boiling water, and reduce the heat to low.
  3. Gradually add ragi flour to the center without stirring initially.
  4. Cover and let it cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
  5. After that, stir vigorously with a wooden stick or ladle to mix everything well into a thick mass without lumps.
  6. Cover and cook again for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Switch off the flame and allow it to cool slightly.
  8. Wet your palms with water, take portions, and roll them into smooth balls.
  9. Serve hot with spicy sambar, saaru, or chutney!
