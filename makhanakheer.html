Makhana Kheer - Creamy Foxnut Pudding

Classic Makhana Kheer Recipe

Makhana Kheer is a creamy and delicious dessert made with foxnuts (makhana), milk, and dry fruits. This traditional Bihari sweet is commonly prepared during festivals and fasting days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups makhana (foxnuts)
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and roast makhana until they are crisp. Let them cool, then coarsely crush half of them.
  2. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan and let it simmer until it thickens slightly.
  3. Add crushed and whole makhana to the milk and cook for 10–15 minutes on low heat.
  4. Add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron, and chopped nuts. Cook until the kheer reaches desired consistency.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.
