Traditional Avial Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1/2 cup beans, cut
  • 1/2 cup ash gourd (white pumpkin), cubed
  • 1/2 cup raw banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup drumsticks (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd), whisked
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • A few curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil the vegetables with turmeric and salt until tender but not mushy.
  2. Grind coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a coarse paste.
  3. Add the paste to the cooked vegetables and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat and add the whisked curd. Mix well gently.
  5. Drizzle coconut oil and add curry leaves on top.
  6. Serve warm with steamed rice.
