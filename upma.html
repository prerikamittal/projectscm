South Indian Upma Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Dry roast semolina in a pan until light golden. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add green chilies, ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  6. Pour in water and add salt. Bring it to a boil.
  7. Slowly add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously.
  8. Stir well to avoid lumps and cook on low heat until water is absorbed.
  9. Cover and steam for 2 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
