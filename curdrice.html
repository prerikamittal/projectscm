Curd Rice

Curd Rice is a simple and comforting South Indian dish made with cooked rice and curd, seasoned with spices. It's perfect for hot days and easy on the stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Mash the cooked rice and mix with curd. Add salt and mix well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, green chilies, ginger, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Pour the tempering over the rice-curd mixture and mix.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve chilled or at room temperature.
