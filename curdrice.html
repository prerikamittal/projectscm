Classic Curd Rice Recipe

Curd Rice (Thayir Sadam) is a simple, comforting South Indian dish made with cooked rice, yogurt (curd), and a tempering of spices. It's cooling and perfect for hot weather.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (soft and mushy)
  • 1 cup curd (plain yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil or ghee for tempering

Instructions:

  1. Mash the cooked rice while it is still warm.
  2. Once slightly cool, add curd and milk. Mix well until creamy.
  3. In a small pan, heat oil/ghee and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add green chilies, ginger, curry leaves, and a pinch of hing. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  5. Pour the tempering over the curd rice mixture. Add salt and mix everything well.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature, optionally with pickle or papad.
