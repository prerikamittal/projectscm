Classic Medu Vada Recipe

Medu Vada is a crispy, savory donut made from urad dal (black gram). It's soft on the inside and crispy on the outside, often served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 cup rice (optional for crispiness)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, finely grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal and rice (if using) in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked dal and rice into a smooth batter. The batter should be thick.
  3. Mix in onions, green chilies, ginger, curry leaves, coriander leaves, and salt.
  4. Heat oil. Shape batter into a ring using wet hands and fry until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
