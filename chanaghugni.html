Chana Ghugni - Spicy and Tangy Chickpea Snack

Classic Chana Ghugni Recipe

Chana Ghugni is a popular Eastern Indian snack made with dried yellow peas or black chickpeas, spiced with onions, garlic, and aromatic masalas. It’s commonly enjoyed with puffed rice or flatbreads.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried yellow peas or black chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander and lemon juice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the soaked peas until soft but not mushy. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and garlic, sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add boiled peas and mix well. Cook for 5–7 minutes until flavors are absorbed.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander and lemon juice before serving.
