Sarson da Saag - Punjabi Winter Favorite

Classic Sarson da Saag Recipe

Sarson da Saag is a traditional Punjabi dish made with mustard greens, commonly served with Makki di Roti. Rich in flavor and nutrition, it's perfect for winter meals.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Sarson (mustard greens)
  • 250g Palak (spinach)
  • 100g Bathua (optional)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1-inch ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp maize flour (makki ka atta)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp ghee or butter

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop all greens. Pressure cook with green chilies, ginger, and garlic for 3-4 whistles.
  2. Let it cool, then blend to a coarse paste.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, saut√© onions till golden, add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add the blended greens. Stir in maize flour and cook on low for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Add salt and more ghee if needed. Serve hot with Makki di Roti!
