Rasam

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tsp rasam powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1 tsp ghee or oil

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, mix tomato, turmeric, tamarind paste, rasam powder, salt, and water.
  2. Bring to a gentle boil for 8-10 minutes.
  3. In a small pan, heat ghee/oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add red chilies, crushed garlic, and curry leaves. Saut√© briefly.
  5. Pour this tempering over the rasam and mix well.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with rice.
