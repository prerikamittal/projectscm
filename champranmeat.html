Champaran Meat

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 1.5 hrs

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg mutton (bone-in)
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup mustard oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon)

Instructions:

  1. Heat mustard oil in a heavy-bottomed pot and temper with whole spices.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in garlic and ginger paste, cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add all powdered spices and salt, cook until oil separates.
  5. Add mutton pieces, mix well to coat with spices.
  6. Cook covered on low heat for 1 to 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. The mutton should be tender and coated in thick masala.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
