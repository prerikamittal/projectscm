Dal Peetha - Steamed Dumplings Stuffed with Lentils

Classic Dal Peetha Recipe

Dal Peetha is a healthy and savory traditional Bihari dish made with rice flour dough stuffed with a spiced lentil filling and typically steamed. It’s often enjoyed with chutney or pickles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram), soaked and ground coarsely
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds. Add onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste and sauté.
  2. Add ground dal, turmeric, salt and cook until the mixture is dry. Add coriander and set aside to cool.
  3. Boil 1.5 cups water with a pinch of salt. Add rice flour slowly, stirring continuously to form a dough.
  4. Let the dough cool slightly, then knead it smooth. Make small balls and flatten them.
  5. Place a spoonful of filling in the center and fold the dough over to seal.
  6. Steam the dumplings for 10–15 minutes or until cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or spicy pickles.
