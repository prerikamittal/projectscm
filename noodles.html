Stir-Fried Veg Noodles

This stir-fried noodle dish is a flavorful and satisfying Asian recipe made with mixed vegetables, soy sauce, garlic, and spring onions. A quick and delicious meal perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup sliced vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add boiled noodles, soy sauce, and chili sauce.
  6. Toss everything well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious stir-fried noodles!
