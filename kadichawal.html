Kadi Chawal - North Indian Comfort Food

Classic Kadi Chawal Recipe

Kadi Chawal is a comforting North Indian dish made with spiced yogurt-based curry and served with steamed rice. It's a delicious and wholesome meal enjoyed across many Indian households.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1-2 dry red chilies
  • Curry leaves (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the yogurt and gram flour together with 2-3 cups of water to make a smooth mixture.
  2. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well and keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, dry red chilies, and curry leaves.
  4. Once they splutter, pour in the yogurt mixture and bring it to a boil while stirring continuously.
  5. Lower the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the kadhi thickens and the raw taste of besan disappears.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice for a delicious traditional meal.
