Delicious Veg Kurma Recipe

Veg Kurma is a flavorful South Indian curry made with a blend of mixed vegetables, coconut, and spices. It is creamy, aromatic, and pairs well with dosa, chapati, or rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut (grated)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Water as required

Instructions:

  1. Grind coconut, green chilies, and fennel seeds into a smooth paste using water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add bay leaf, cloves, and cinnamon.
  3. Saute onions till translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mix well.
  6. Add chopped vegetables and mix.
  7. Pour water, cover, and cook till vegetables are soft.
  8. Add the coconut paste and garam masala.
  9. Cook for another 5-7 minutes until the curry thickens.
  10. Serve hot with chapati, dosa, or rice!
