South Indian Kootu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 2 cups chopped mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, pumpkin, etc.)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 dried red chili
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 tsp oil

Preparation Steps:

  • Cook moong dal with turmeric powder until soft.
  • Boil chopped vegetables until tender but not mushy.
  • Grind coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a coarse paste.
  • Mix cooked dal, vegetables, ground coconut paste, and salt. Simmer for a few minutes.
  • Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, dried red chili, and curry leaves.
  • Pour the tempering over the kootu and mix well.
  • Serve hot with rice or chapati!
