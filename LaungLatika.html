Laung-Latika - Bihari Delight

Traditional Laung-Latika Recipe

Laung-Latika is a popular Bihari sweet made with maida dough filled with khoya, sealed with a clove (laung), and deep-fried to golden perfection before being dipped in sugar syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Water (to knead)
  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 2 tbsp chopped dry fruits
  • Cardamom powder (a pinch)
  • Cloves (laung) for sealing
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)

Instructions:

  1. Knead a soft dough with flour, ghee, and water. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix khoya, dry fruits, and cardamom for the filling.
  3. Divide dough, roll into discs, place filling, fold into a parcel, and seal with a clove.
  4. Deep-fry on low flame until golden brown.
  5. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water to 1-string consistency.
  6. Dip fried Laung-Latikas in warm syrup and let them soak for a few minutes before serving.
