Uttapam

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli/dosa batter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat a tawa or non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter.
  2. Spread slightly to make a thick pancake.
  3. Sprinkle onion, tomato, chili, and coriander on top.
  4. Drizzle oil around the sides and cook on medium flame.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
