Classic Uttapam Recipe

Uttapam is a soft and thick savory South Indian pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter, topped with vegetables. It is hearty, filling, and usually served with sambar or chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dosa batter (fermented)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Stir the dosa batter gently and adjust salt if needed.
  2. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa and drizzle a little oil.
  3. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it gently to form a thick pancake.
  4. Sprinkle chopped onion, tomato, green chili, and coriander leaves on top.
  5. Drizzle a bit of oil around the edges and on top of the veggies.
  6. Cook on medium heat until the bottom turns golden brown and the top is cooked.
  7. Flip and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes (optional).
  8. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
