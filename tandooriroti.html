Tandoori Roti - Punjabi Delight

Classic Tandoori Roti Recipe

Tandoori Roti is a soft, chewy flatbread traditionally cooked in a tandoor (clay oven). It's a staple in Punjabi cuisine and goes perfectly with rich curries like paneer butter masala or dal makhani.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to knead)
  • Butter or ghee for brushing

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add yogurt and start kneading the dough, adding water gradually as needed. Knead into a smooth, soft dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat a tawa (griddle) or oven. Divide dough into equal balls and roll into rotis.
  5. Place roti on the hot tawa. Once bubbles appear, flip and cook the other side. Optionally place directly on flame for a charred effect.
  6. Brush with butter or ghee and serve hot.
  7. Pairs great with paneer dishes, dal, or chole.
