Delicious Veg Manchurian Recipe

Veg Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with deep-fried vegetable balls tossed in a spicy and tangy soy-based sauce. It can be served as an appetizer or with fried rice/noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrot, grated
  • 1/4 cup capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix cabbage, carrot, capsicum, all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and pepper. Form into small balls.
  2. Deep-fry the balls on medium heat until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil. Add garlic and ginger, and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and a bit of water. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Add cornflour slurry (1 tsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp water) to thicken the sauce.
  6. Add the fried balls and toss well to coat with the sauce.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot!
